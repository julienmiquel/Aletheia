## 2025-01-22 - Visual Forensics Color Contrast
**Learning:** The visual forensics section uses green/yellow/red/purple highlighting behind white text (if default color is white or depending on theme). For light colors like yellow and green, white text is poorly visible, and even black text might have low contrast depending on the specific shades. The app relies on `#d4edda` (Light Green), `#fff3cd` (Light Yellow), `#f8d7da` (Light Red), and `#e2e3e5` (Light Purple) which all require dark text for accessible contrast ratios. Currently it relies on the default Streamlit text color, which might be white in dark mode.
**Action:** Explicitly set text color to dark gray or black (`#212529`) inside the colored token spans to ensure consistent contrast regardless of Streamlit theme.
